FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

Our MOQ is flexible and depends on the product type. For standard products, we can accept small sample orders. For OEM/ODM orders with custom branding, MOQ typically starts from 10–50 units. Contact us and we'll work with your requirements.

Do you offer OEM/ODM services?

Yes. We offer full OEM/ODM services including custom size, bezel design, logo branding, OS configuration, interface customization, and packaging. Please share your requirements and we'll provide a tailored proposal.

What is included with the CMS software?

Every display purchase includes our professional cloud-based CMS at no extra cost. Features include remote content management, scheduling, one-click publishing, built-in templates, multi-user access, and playback analytics. There are no monthly subscription fees.

What certifications do your products have?

Our displays are CE, FCC, and RoHS certified. Indoor products meet standard safety requirements for European and US markets. Outdoor products are IP65 rated. We can provide certification documentation upon request.

What is the production and delivery lead time?

Standard products typically ship within 7–15 business days. Custom OEM/ODM orders are 20–35 business days depending on specifications and quantity. We ship globally via sea freight (FOB Shenzhen) or air freight.

What warranty do you offer?

All products come with a standard 12-month warranty covering manufacturing defects. Extended warranty options are available. Our after-sales team provides remote technical support and replacement parts for the warranty period.

Can I visit your factory?

Yes, factory visits are welcome. Our factory is located in Shenzhen, China. Please contact us in advance to arrange your visit and we'll prepare a full product demonstration for you.

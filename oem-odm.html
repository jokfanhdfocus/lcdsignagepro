Why OEM with HDFocus

What Does OEM Digital Signage Manufacturing Actually Mean?

OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) digital signage means we manufacture the hardware under your brand. Your logo appears on the bezel, packaging, boot screen, and CMS interface — your customers never see "HDFocus". ODM (Original Design Manufacturer) goes further: we co-develop custom hardware form factors, enclosures, or chipset configurations specifically for your product roadmap.

Most digital signage distributors, system integrators, and resellers source OEM from HDFocus because our Grade-A panels, in-house R&D team, and dedicated QC line ensure consistent quality at scale — without the capital investment of owning a factory.

Common Questions

OEM/ODM Digital Signage FAQ

Answers to the questions we hear most from distributors, resellers, and brand owners.

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ) for OEM digital signage?
HDFocus accepts OEM orders from as low as 10 units for standard product lines (branding only). For fully custom ODM designs requiring new tooling or mold, MOQ is typically 50–100 units depending on the complexity of the customization.
Can I put my own brand on HDFocus digital signage products?
Yes, absolutely. We provide full white-label OEM services including custom logo on the bezel and back panel, brand colors, custom packaging, boot screen animation, and CMS branding. Your customers will only ever see your brand — never HDFocus.
How long does OEM digital signage production take?
Standard OEM orders (branding and packaging only, no hardware changes) typically ship within 15–20 business days after sample approval. Custom ODM hardware designs require 45–60 days for tooling, prototyping, and first sample production before mass production begins.
Do HDFocus OEM products come with CE and FCC certification?
Yes. All HDFocus products are CE, FCC, and RoHS certified as standard. For specific market requirements — such as CB (international), SAA (Australia), SASO (Saudi Arabia), or BIS (India) — we can arrange additional testing and certification as part of your OEM package.
Will HDFocus sign a non-disclosure agreement (NDA)?
Yes. We routinely sign NDAs with OEM partners to protect your product designs, business plans, and customer relationships. Our standard NDA covers IP ownership, manufacturing exclusivity, and confidentiality for a period of 5 years.
Can HDFocus pre-load my software or CMS onto the devices?
Yes. We support pre-loading any Android APK, Windows application, or enterprise MDM profile before shipment. Our engineering team can also integrate third-party APIs, disable system apps, and configure custom Android launchers to create a fully locked-down branded device experience.

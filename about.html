Who We Are

Your Trusted LCD Signage Manufacturing Partner

HDFocus is a professional manufacturer of LCD digital signage solutions, headquartered in Shenzhen, China — the heart of the global electronics industry. We specialize in designing, manufacturing, and supplying high-quality digital displays for B2B clients across retail, hospitality, food & beverage, corporate, and outdoor environments.

With a full product lineup ranging from sleek indoor displays to robust outdoor kiosks, we serve distribution partners, system integrators, and enterprise clients across Europe, North America, Southeast Asia, and the Middle East.
